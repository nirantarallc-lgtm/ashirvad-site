Your right to delete your data is real and unconditional. We make it simple, and we confirm when it’s done.

You have the right to delete your Pooja Now account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to do it, what happens when you do, and what data is retained by legal requirement.

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account, profile, and personal data cannot be recovered. Please read the information below before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Pooja Now app on your device.
  2. Go to your Profile (tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation).
  3. Scroll down to Account Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted.

Your account will be scheduled for deletion immediately. You will be signed out of the App and your personal data will be erased within 30 days, except for data we are required to retain by law (see below).

Cooling-Off Period

We know that deletion requests can sometimes be made in haste. After you confirm deletion in the App:

  • Your account enters a 24-hour cooling-off period during which it is deactivated but not yet permanently deleted.
  • If you change your mind, you can log back in within 24 hours to cancel the deletion request.
  • After 24 hours, deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Can’t Access the App?

If you cannot access your account or the App, you can request deletion by email:

  1. Email us at support-poojanow@nirantara.online
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered mobile number or email address so we can identify your account.

We will acknowledge your request within 24 hours and process the deletion within 7 business days, confirming via email when complete.

What Gets Deleted

DataAction on Deletion
Your profile (name, contact details)Permanently deleted within 30 days
Authentication credentials (mobile / email)Permanently deleted within 30 days
Saved addressesPermanently deleted within 30 days
Session tokens and cached dataInvalidated immediately on deletion
Reviews submitted by youAnonymised — review text may be retained but your name is permanently removed
Booking and payment recordsRetained for 5 years (statutory financial record requirement under the Income Tax Act and GST regulations), then permanently deleted

About Review Anonymisation

Reviews you have written are a contribution to the community. When your account is deleted, your name and profile are permanently removed from all reviews. The review text itself may be retained in anonymised form (e.g., “Verified Devotee”) to maintain the integrity of the pandit rating system. You will not be identifiable from the retained content.

Booking and payment records are retained for 5 years as required under Indian financial regulations (GST and Income Tax Act). These records contain booking details and payment amounts only — they do not contain payment card numbers, UPI IDs, or CVV details. After the statutory retention period, all records are permanently and securely deleted.

For Pandits

If you are a registered pandit and wish to delete your account, please be aware of the following before proceeding:

  • Any pending payouts will be processed to your linked bank account before your Razorpay linked account is closed. This may take up to 5–7 business days.
  • Your pandit profile will be removed from search results immediately upon deletion confirmation.
  • Any active or upcoming bookings must be completed or cancelled before account deletion can proceed. If you have active bookings, contact us first at support-poojanow@nirantara.online and we will coordinate the process.
  • Your pandit earnings records will be retained for 5 years as required by law, after which they are permanently deleted.
