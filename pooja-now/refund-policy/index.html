This policy explains when and how you can cancel a pooja booking and receive a refund. By completing a booking on Pooja Now, you agree to this policy.

Understanding the Charges

A Pooja Now booking total comprises up to three components:

  • Pooja charges — The base fee for the religious service, set by Nirantara Labs based on the pooja type and region. This is what the pandit is paid for their time and service (minus platform commission).
  • Muhurat premium — An additional charge applied when you select an auspicious time slot (muhurat). This reflects increased pandit demand for those slots.
  • Platform convenience fee — 5% of the pooja charges, capped at ₹151. This covers booking management, customer support, and platform infrastructure costs. It is non-refundable in all cases.

1. Cancellation by the Devotee

Cancellation Timing Refund on Pooja Charges Refund on Muhurat Premium
More than 24 hours before the scheduled pooja 100% refund 100% refund
Between 12 and 24 hours before the scheduled pooja 50% refund No refund
Less than 12 hours before the scheduled pooja No refund No refund
No-show (devotee absent at scheduled time) No refund No refund
The platform convenience fee is non-refundable in all cases, as it covers costs incurred at the time of booking regardless of cancellation.

2. Cancellation by the Pandit

If a pandit cancels a confirmed booking for any reason:

  • You will receive a full refund of all charges including the convenience fee and any muhurat premium.
  • We will make best efforts to find a replacement pandit for the same date and time at no additional cost.
  • If no replacement is available, we will notify you immediately so you can rebook at your convenience.

3. Rescheduling a Booking

If you need to change the date or time of your booking:

  • Rescheduling is allowed if requested more than 24 hours before the original scheduled time, subject to pandit availability.
  • Contact us at support-poojanow@nirantara.online with your booking ID and preferred new date/time.
  • Rescheduling is not guaranteed and depends on the pandit’s availability. If rescheduling is not possible, your standard cancellation rights apply.
  • If you reschedule to a muhurat slot, the additional muhurat premium must be paid at that time.
  • Rescheduling within 24 hours of the original booking time is treated as a late cancellation and the standard cancellation refund rules apply.

4. Service Not Rendered / Quality Issues

If the pandit fails to arrive, arrives significantly late, or the service is materially different from what was described and booked:

  • Report the issue within 48 hours of the scheduled pooja time via the App or by emailing support-poojanow@nirantara.online.
  • Include your booking ID and a brief description of what went wrong. Supporting evidence (photographs, description) may be requested.
  • We will investigate the complaint promptly. Where the complaint is upheld, we will issue a full or partial refund at our discretion, and take appropriate action with respect to the pandit.
  • Complaints raised after 48 hours may not be eligible for a refund but will still be investigated for platform quality purposes.

5. How to Cancel

  1. Open the Pooja Now app on your device.
  2. Go to My Bookings.
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel.
  4. Tap Cancel Booking and confirm your decision.

Alternatively, email support-poojanow@nirantara.online with your booking ID and we will process the cancellation for you. Include the subject: Booking Cancellation Request.

6. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method (credit/debit card, UPI, net banking, etc.).
  • Refunds are initiated by us within 2 business days of approval.
  • It may take an additional 5–10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • All refunds are processed by Razorpay on our behalf. You will receive a Razorpay refund confirmation once the refund is initiated.

7. Convenience Fee

The platform convenience fee (5% of pooja charges, capped at ₹151) is non-refundable in all cases. This fee covers booking management, payment processing, and platform infrastructure costs that are incurred at the time of booking, regardless of whether the booking is later cancelled.

8. Promo Codes and Discounts

  • If a promo code was applied to your booking, the refund amount will reflect the actual amount paid after the discount — not the pre-discount price.
  • Promo codes are not reinstated after a cancellation.
  • Promo credits issued by Nirantara Labs (e.g., as goodwill gestures) are non-transferable and expire as stated at the time of issuance.

9. Abuse Protection

Policy abuse will result in account action. Repeated cancellations, fabricated quality complaints, or coordinated misuse of our refund policy (such as booking-and-cancelling to game promo codes) may result in temporary or permanent suspension of your account. We reserve the right to decline refund requests where evidence of systematic abuse is identified.
