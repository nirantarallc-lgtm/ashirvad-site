4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. When you make a payment:

  • Your card or UPI details are entered directly into Razorpay’s secure checkout. Pooja Now never sees or stores your card number, CVV, or UPI PIN.
  • Pooja Now stores only the Razorpay order ID, payment ID, and payment status for booking records.
  • Pandit payouts are processed automatically via Razorpay Route (split payment). The platform retains a commission and transfers the pandit’s share to their verified linked account.

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Cloud database (Supabase) — Account, profile, bookings, and payment records are stored on Supabase (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL on AWS infrastructure) with row-level security enabled. Each user can only access their own data.
  • On your device — Session tokens and cached profile data are stored locally using AsyncStorage (React Native). This data is cleared when you log out.
  • Firebase (Google) — Used for SMS OTP delivery only. We do not store Firebase user profiles beyond the authentication step.

5.2 Security Measures

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) in our database ensures each user can only read and write their own records.
  • Payment processing meets PCI-DSS standards via Razorpay.
  • Pandit KYC and financial data is handled entirely by Razorpay’s regulated infrastructure.

8. Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), you have the right to:

  • Access your data — View your profile and booking history within the App at any time.
  • Correct your data — Update your name, address, or contact details in the App settings.
  • Delete your account — Permanently delete your account and personal data. See our Account Deletion page for full instructions. Booking and payment records are retained for 5 years as required by law.
  • Withdraw consent — Stop using the App at any time and request account deletion.
  • Nominate a representative — Under the DPDP Act, you may nominate another person to exercise your rights on your behalf.

To exercise any of the above rights, contact: support-poojanow@nirantara.online. We will acknowledge your request within 72 hours and complete it within 30 days.
