NourishIQ — Privacy Policy

Food Safety Scanner

Effective Date: March 23, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 23, 2026

Nirantara Labs (“we,” “us,” or “our”), based in Pune, Maharashtra, India, operates the NourishIQ mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using NourishIQ, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms of Use, which govern your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign up using Google or Apple authentication, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Full name (as provided by your OAuth provider)

We do not collect or store your OAuth provider password.

1.2 Profile & Preferences

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Language preference (English or Hindi)
  • Health mode (e.g., default, parent, diabetic, allergy, fitness)
  • Regulation country preference (e.g., India/FSSAI, EU/EFSA, US/FDA — used to show country-specific regulatory status for ingredients)
  • Allergy information (if you choose to enter allergies for personalised warnings)

1.3 Usage Data

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Scan history — products you scan (barcode, product name, timestamp, language used at time of scan)
  • Saved and avoid lists — products you mark as “saved” or “avoid”
  • Missing product reports — if a scanned barcode is not in our database, the barcode is logged to help us expand our product catalogue

1.4 Device Permissions

  • Camera — used solely to scan product barcodes. Camera data is processed on-device and is not stored, recorded, or transmitted. You may deny camera access and enter barcodes manually instead.

1.5 Subscription & Payment Data

  • We verify your subscription status through Apple App Store or Google Play Store receipt validation.
  • We store only your subscription status (active/expired) and expiry date.
  • We do not store payment card details, billing addresses, or purchase receipts. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google.

1.6 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: device identifiers or advertising IDs, location data, contacts, photos or files, browsing history, or any analytics/crash reporting data. No third-party analytics SDKs are used.

3. AI-Powered Analysis (Anthropic Claude)

When you scan a product, the App generates a safety analysis using Anthropic’s Claude AI service. The following data is sent to Anthropic’s servers for analysis:

  • Product ingredient list (INS codes, ingredient names, risk classifications, regulatory status)
  • Your health mode (e.g., “parent,” “diabetic”) — to personalise the safety report
The following is NOT sent to Anthropic: your name, email, or any personal identifiers; your scan history or saved lists; your allergy list (only the health mode category is sent).

AI-generated analysis results are cached on our servers for up to 7 days to reduce repeated processing. Anthropic’s use of data is governed by their own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Cloud database — Your account, profile, scan history, and lists are stored on Supabase (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL) with row-level security enforced. You can only access your own data.
  • On your device — Authentication state, language preference, and onboarding status are stored locally using AsyncStorage.
  • Product database — Public product and ingredient data is also stored locally on your device in an SQLite database for offline access.

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Cloud database access uses Row-Level Security (RLS) — each user can only read and write their own profile, scans, and lists.
  • Public data (product information, ingredient safety data, regulatory data) is read-only and does not contain personal information.
  • OAuth authentication is handled by Supabase Auth using industry-standard protocols.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — View your profile, scan history, and lists within the App.
  • Correct your data — Update your profile information (language, health mode, allergies) at any time in the App.
  • Delete your account — Permanently delete your account and all associated data (profile, scan history, saved/avoid lists) from within the App’s Profile screen. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Deny camera access — Use manual barcode entry instead.
  • Withdraw consent — Stop using the App and delete your account at any time.

To exercise any right not available in-app, or for any privacy-related questions, contact us at nourishiq.support@nirantara.online.

8. Children’s Privacy

NourishIQ is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The “Parent” health mode is designed for parents and guardians to check food safety for their children — it is not intended for use by children themselves.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at nourishiq.support@nirantara.online and we will promptly delete the account and associated data.
